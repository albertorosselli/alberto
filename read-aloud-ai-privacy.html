Read Aloud AI

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 13, 2025

TL;DR: Read Aloud AI does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your privacy is 100% protected.

1. Introduction

Read Aloud AI is a browser extension that provides text-to-speech functionality for web content. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles (or more accurately, does not handle) your data.

2. Data Collection

Read Aloud AI does NOT collect any data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not collect the text you select to read aloud
  • We do not collect usage statistics or analytics
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect IP addresses or device information

3. How the Extension Works

Read Aloud AI operates entirely within your browser using the browser's built-in Web Speech API:

  • Text Selection: When you select text and activate the extension, the text is processed locally in your browser
  • Speech Synthesis: The text is converted to speech using your browser's native Web Speech API
  • Settings Storage: Your preferences (voice selection, speed, volume, pitch) are stored locally in your browser using the browser's storage API
  • No External Servers: No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties

4. Permissions Explained

The extension requests certain permissions to function. Here's what each permission is used for:

  • activeTab: Access the text you select on the current webpage to read it aloud
  • contextMenus: Add "Read Aloud" option to your right-click menu
  • scripting: Inject the content script that enables text-to-speech on web pages
  • storage: Save your preferences (voice, speed, volume, pitch) locally in your browser
  • Host permissions (<all_urls>): Allow the extension to work on any website you visit

Important: These permissions are used solely for the extension's functionality. No data accessed through these permissions is collected, stored externally, or transmitted.

5. Local Storage

The only data stored by Read Aloud AI is your preferences:

  • Selected voice
  • Speech rate (speed)
  • Volume level
  • Pitch setting

This data is stored locally in your browser using the browser's storage API and never leaves your device.

6. Third-Party Services

Read Aloud AI does not use any third-party services. The extension does not:

  • Connect to external APIs
  • Use analytics services (no Google Analytics, no tracking)
  • Include advertising networks
  • Communicate with any external servers

7. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not collect any data. Your information remains entirely private and local to your device.

8. Children's Privacy

Read Aloud AI does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is safe for users of all ages.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

10. Open Source

Your privacy is inherently protected by our no-data-collection approach.
